How to take the typing test
  1. Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  2. Click Start to load a sentence and enable the typing box.
  3. Type the sentence shown. Live highlights show correct/incorrect words.
  4. Press Enter when you finish typing to stop the timer and lock the box.
  5. Review your results: Level, Time, and WPM.
  6. Click Retry for a new sentence at the same difficulty, then continue practicing.

Tip: Focus on accuracy first; speed will follow. You can change difficulty anytime before starting.
